What are Terpenes?

Terpenes are essential oils responsible for the distinct aromas, flavors, and colors of plants including hemp! They are what give the Tange OG strain its citrus and tropical taste and Strawberry AK its sweet and fruity flavor, but terpenes do more than just deliver delicious flavor. They also determine how a particular strain will affect your body and mind thus having a direct effect on your whole CBD experience!

The best part? When combined with terpenes, CBD oil delivers a richer experience by producing a synergistic phenomenon known as The Entourage Effect. In other words, terpenes and CBD work better together than they do individually, like a team of superheroes combining forces to improve your overall health and wellbeing.
